# Embeddable, Interactive Charts Now Released

We’ve released embeddable interactive charts on Lychee.

This means you can publish a chart to a public URL and embed it directly inside articles, guides, or other pages while keeping the chart interactive. Hover states, tooltips, and chart behavior are preserved, so readers can explore the data instead of seeing a static image.

Why this is useful:

- your readers can inspect exact values directly in your post
- your chart can stay up to date from the same saved chart source
- sharing is simpler because one chart URL can be reused across channels

## How to Embed on Your Site

Use these same steps for WordPress, Webflow, Ghost, Framer, Wix, custom HTML sites, and any platform that supports iframe embeds.

1. In Lychee, open your chart and go to **Export**.
2. In **Share**, choose a slug and click **Publish embed**.
3. Copy the iframe snippet.
4. In your website editor, add an **HTML/Embed** block and paste the snippet.
5. Publish your page.

The chart stays interactive everywhere it is embedded (tooltips, hover states, and animations).

## Share Raw URL vs Embed

- **Raw URL** (`https://lycheedata.com/<handle>/charts/<slug>`): best for social platforms like X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Slack, or Discord. The preview card uses the chart OG image.
- **Iframe embed**: best for blog posts, docs, landing pages, and CMS pages where you want the fully interactive chart inline.
