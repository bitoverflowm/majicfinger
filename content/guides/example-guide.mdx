## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A Lychee account
- Access to your dashboard
- Basic familiarity with data visualization

## Initial Setup

Follow these steps to configure your first project:

1. Log in to your Lychee dashboard
2. Create a new project from the dashboard
3. Connect your first integration (e.g., Stripe or Twitter)
4. Configure your data sources
